Daniel Nima Moattar | editor, reporter, researcher

I am the research editor and fellowship coordinator at Mother Jones, and an investigative journalist with work in The Guardian, The Nation, Salon, and other outlets.
I edit for print and web, report on public and private malfeasance, direct research teams, and train early-career reporters. Journalism belongs to all of us.

[reporting]

    I've investigated conservative astroturfing, Chicago school closures, and chemical weapons in prisons. I focus on:
  • labor (The Nation, Salon),
  • business (In These Times),
  • borders (The Guardian, The Nation),
  • prisons (DW Online, The American Prospect),
  • and movement politics (VICE, In These Times), nationally, in Chicagoland, and in the San Francisco Bay Area.
